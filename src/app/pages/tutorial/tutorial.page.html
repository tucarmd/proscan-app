Turn ignition to the ON position. Do not start the vehicle. Plug tool in to the DLC Port.

Enter Mileage and select Start Scan

If CarMD Pro Scan fails to automatically grab the VIN then scan the VIN

Select tool if multiple are detected

Tool will begin scanning vehicle. This process may take several minutes

The report summary can be viewed on the app. Here you will have the option to erase all or individual codes. Your full report will be available on the web portal

You can go back and view your complete or incomplete past reports through the home screen by selecting Past Reports
